COVID-19

We are proud to provide your family with a safe and exciting activity even during these challenging times. To ensure that the tour remains safe for everyone, we require all participants to be fully-vaccinated and wear a mask during the indoor part of our tours. If you experience COVID-19 Symptoms within 10 days of your tour, please give us a call and we will reschedule your tour for a later date at no extra charge.
