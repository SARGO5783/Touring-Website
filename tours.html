Yellowstone National Park


The adventure of a life time! Join our one and only backpacking tour that takes tourists across the Rocky Mountains and through the entire Yellowstone National Park! Watch pictures from the books come alive as you explore the most beautiful mountains, flowing streams, and cliffs that drop thousands of miles near your feet. We provide all necessary equipment and food for the travels. Each tour begins in Denver, Colorado and completes in Cheyenne, Wyoming! Book quickly as this tour sells out fast!

California Grand Tour


Join us for an incredible four day adventure along the Western Coast. Take a peek into Hollywood, sit along the Western beaches, and tour the Golden Gate Bridge, amongst other adventures! Sail along San Francisco's breathtaking waterfront, under the majestic Golden Gate Bridge, and around notorious Alcatraz Island. Discover San Francisco's history via an award-winning audio commentary, available in 16 languages.

Grand Canyon


Fly past the Rocky mountains to America's world famous- Grand Canyon. Hike within the Arizona desert,watch sunsets by the Grand Canyon, and experiance the beauty of America! Truly, the Grand Canyon is one of the most spectacular and biggest sites on Earth. To discover as much as you can of the Grand Canyon, including the rich stories, history, and wildlife- you will want to join our guided tour. Because with facts and figures this staggeringly impressive, you might have a couple of questions needing answering!

Niagara Falls


See what makes the Niagara Falls a #1 Tourist attraction! Ride beneath the falls, explore the caves, and watch as thousands of pounds of water crash down the falls daily. Ride the Niagara Scenic Trolley for a historic overview and fun facts & tips while enjoying your stay, or stay and watch a short film by Academy Award-winning producer Keith Merrill in our Adventure Theater, which delivers a captivating story of Niagara Falls explorers and daredevils. Along side the falls, visit the many delicious cafes and hike along the top of falls.
